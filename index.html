weightlift Mobile App

Work in progress

The Weightlift App is designed to help you keep track of your progress in the gym while lifting weights.

Getting started with this app is simple. Sign up with name, username and password. Check out all the exercises. Swipe left to inspect and/or edit existing exercises or add a new if you dont find your favourite exercise. When you start your workout session just press on "Add new workout". When you are done with your workout session press "Finish" and your workout will immediately be saved in the database attached to your acccount so you can easily browse trough your workout history.

  • Easy registration.
  • Sign in with username and password.
  • Most common exercises are already stored in the database.
  • Browse trough your workout history.

The application dynamically switches between dark and light mode based on your Phone theme-settings.

Techniques, patterns and frameworks used

  • .NET/C#
  • Xamarin
  • Xamarin forms
  • MVVM pattern
  • Express Node.js
  • MongoDB

    Alumnsystem

    Alumnsystem is a windows desktop application developed in Windows Presentation Foundation (WPF). Former students who have graduated can with the help of this application keep in touch with the university and their former teachers. The current version saves all the data to a local relational database and uses LINQ to talk to the database. But one could easily connect a database (thanks to Entity Framework) that is available in the cloud for global access to the stored data.

    The employees of the university can sign in and publish diffrent events, activites or campaigns. The publisher can decide wich alumns can sign up and attend to the published event. A logged in employee can also see wich students are attending to an event. Also, a logged in employee can send messages to specific group of alumns.

    A logged in alumn can easily get an overview of wich events he or she have access to and wich events he or she have signed up for.
